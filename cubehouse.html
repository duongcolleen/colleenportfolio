Cube House

First Year Semester 1 Final Studio Project

The goal of this project was to solve a space making problem. The cube pavillion is 20' x 20' x 20' The site includes: A Departure Parking, Arrival Procession Space, A Ceremony Viewing Garden, A Reception Garden Space with Tables and Seat, A Meal Serving Space, A Restroom, and Storage.
